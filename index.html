How EcoVerse Works

Explore the key modules designed to facilitate municipal cleanup and community greening.

Plant a Tree & Get Points

Log your new sapling with our camera simulation. Our AI confirms the species and tags coordinates to place it on the municipal forestry map. Retain reminders to earn points.

Plant sapling

Garbage Spot Reporting

Take pictures of scattered waste in public lanes. Geotagged coordinates submit the cleanup request to the Municipal Corporation. Tracking logs updates citizens on status changes.

Report trash

Authorized E-Waste recycling

Trace nearby authorized collection facilities. Deposit laptops, batteries, or chargers, scan the center's QR code, and receive Eco Points to swap for transport nursery passes.

Find center

Carbon Footprint Assessment

Assess your monthly carbon footprint. Tweak home energy consumption, travel range, and recycling rates to view how your lifestyle compares to global sustainability objectives.

Open calculator

Flora Encyclopedia & Quizzes

Learn about trees via Wikipedia searches. Test your eco-acumen in our gamified quizzes to unlock badges (Leaf Legend, Grand Eco-Guardian) and earn points.

Open encyclopedia

Emergency Civic Alerts

Report severe municipal leaks, electricity pole sparks, potholes, or sewage blockages directly to the repair dispatch crew. Track the live deployment logs.
